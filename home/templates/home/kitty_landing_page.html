💡 Essential Cat Care Tips

🍽️

Proper Nutrition

Feed your cat high-quality food with the right balance of proteins, fats, and vitamins. Always provide fresh water!

🏠

Safe Environment

Create a cat-friendly space with scratching posts, cozy beds, and plenty of hiding spots for your feline friend.

💊

Regular Vet Visits

Schedule annual check-ups and keep vaccinations up to date to ensure your cat stays healthy and happy.

🧸

Play & Exercise

Engage your cat with interactive toys and play sessions to keep them mentally and physically stimulated.

🤓 Fun Cat Facts

🐾 Cats spend 70% of their lives sleeping

That's 13-16 hours a day! No wonder they're so energetic when they're awake.

👅 Cats have sandpaper tongues

Their tongues are covered in tiny spines called papillae that help them groom and eat.

🏃‍♂️ Cats can run up to 30 mph

They're incredibly fast runners, especially when chasing toys or laser pointers!

🎵 Cats have over 20 muscles in each ear

This allows them to rotate their ears 180 degrees to hear sounds from all directions.
